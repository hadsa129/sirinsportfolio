Who I am

About Me

I'm an Electrical Engineer from the National Engineering School of Sfax (ENIS), specialized in hardware design with a focus on multi-layer PCB design, signal & power integrity, and hardware validation.

My work spans from complex 8-layer System-on-Module boards to IoT gateways integrating multiple wireless technologies. I bring a structured approach to hardware: from simulation-first validation through HyperLynx and LTspice, to manufacturable deliverables (Gerbers, BOM, assembly drawings).

I'm passionate about the intersection of embedded systems and analog/RF design — the place where software meets physics. I've been invited as a speaker at IEEE SSCS workshops, sharing PCB design knowledge with the engineering community.
